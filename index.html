ABOUT THE PURPOSEFUL GENERATION

The Purposeful Generation is a non-governmental organization that reaches out to young secondary school students in their final academic year. Its primary objective is to help young people realize the possibilities within themselves.

As students prepare to face the wider world, it is crucial that they are educated about the challenges ahead, how to overcome them, and how to discover the potential in their environment.
